About AI Makes Possible

AI Makes Possible is a cutting-edge initiative dedicated to advancing the field of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). We're pushing the boundaries of what's technically feasible, combining state-of-the-art machine learning architectures with innovative approaches to cognition and reasoning.

Our interdisciplinary team of researchers, engineers, and ethicists is working collaboratively to develop AI systems that demonstrate human-like understanding, adaptability, and problem-solving capabilities across domains.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To create general artificial intelligence that enhances human creativity, solves complex global challenges, and opens new frontiers of scientific discovery while ensuring these capabilities benefit humanity.

Mission

We're pursuing a step-wise approach to AGI, developing increasingly capable systems with human-aligned values, robust safety guarantees, and transparent decision-making processes. Our multidisciplinary research focuses on multimodal learning, transferable knowledge representation, adaptive reasoning, and alignment techniques.

Research & Innovations

Foundational Models

Our large-scale pre-trained models establish new state-of-the-art benchmarks across language understanding, reasoning, and creative generation tasks.

Multimodal Intelligence

Seamlessly integrating visual, auditory, and textual data streams for comprehensive understanding and generation capabilities.

Causal Reasoning

Developing algorithms that model causal relationships, enabling more robust generalization to new tasks and domains.

Alignment & Safety

Creating innovations in value alignment, interpretability, and robustness to ensure AI systems act according to human intentions.

Roadmap to AGI

2025

Foundation Phase

Deployment of highly capable multimodal models with improved reasoning and planning capabilities.

2027

Integration Phase

Development of systems demonstrating cross-domain transfer learning and generalization.

2029

Emergence Phase

Creation of AI with robust causal reasoning, open-ended problem solving, and self-improvement capabilities.

2032

AGI Achievement

First deployments of general intelligence systems with human-level performance across all cognitive domains.
